Legal & Ethical Guidelines

Educational Purpose: This workshop is designed for educational and training purposes only. All techniques should be used responsibly and legally.

Respect Privacy: Always respect individuals' privacy and organizational security. Only access publicly available information.

Legal Compliance: Accessing systems without authorization is illegal. Viewing publicly indexed data is research; attempting unauthorized access is a crime.

Professional Ethics: Use these skills to improve security, conduct legitimate research, and support lawful investigations.
